    What Are Common Dental Emergencies?

    Toothaches: If you're experiencing severe tooth pain, it could be due to an infection, decay, or even injury. A toothache can be debilitating, and the discomfort can worsen over time. It's important to seek emergency dental care to address the underlying cause of the pain.

    Knocked-Out Tooth: A knocked-out tooth is a true dental emergency. If you act quickly, it may be possible to save the tooth. At Hallandale Emergency Dentist, we have the tools and expertise to handle emergency tooth extraction and re-implantation when necessary.

    Cracked or Broken Teeth: A cracked tooth can cause pain and sensitivity, and a broken tooth can lead to further complications if left untreated. Whether it's a small crack or a complete break, prompt attention is needed to repair the damage and prevent infection.
